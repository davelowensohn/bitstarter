A shuffle button for live music.

Tapemix is a lightweight, cross-platform web app that makes live music mixtapes at the push of a button. It works on desktop, tablet and mobile devices, and is optimized for hands free, on-the-go playback.

Unbelievably easy to use.

1. Choose band

Select a band from the Live Music Archive. There are currently over four thousand to choose from, with tapers uploading hundreds of new shows every month.

2. Set length

Let Tapemix know how much music you want. Maybe you're walking to the store. Maybe you're driving across Canada.

3. Rock out

Tapemix will assemble a seamless concert experience for you, randomized so it's never the same twice.

FAQ


Is [SOME BAND] on Tapemix?
Maybe! Check out the full list of 4500+ participating bands at the Live Music Archive.
You're sure this is legal?
Yep. Our audio files come directly from the Live Music Archive at Archive.org, which only accepts music from bands who have provided written authorization for the recording and circulation of their live performances. As per the Live Music Archive's own FAQ: "Patrons may download from the LMA with the understanding that the artists still hold their copyrights. All material is strictly noncommercial, both for access here and for any further distribution."
Who are you?
Tapemix is the baby pet project of Dave Lowensohn, a musician/coder from the Pacific Northwest who's been musician/coding for the better part of his adult life & thought it would be fun to combine the two. He helms Handwoven Industries, a Portland web design studio which lets him use the word "we." His band Speechwriters LLC somehow has almost a million downloads on archive.org & he's not ashamed to admit that he has like six cubic feet of Phish tapes in a box downstairs.
What's wrong with you that you're asking for bitcoin?
A valid question. One, this is actually the final project for an online course and we get graded on how much bitcoin we're able to raise. Two, if enough money actually comes in, we'll be able to take some time away from paid work and get Tapemix off the ground much quicker than anticipated. To that end, we're also accepting plain old US dollars, but encourage you to use bitcoin if you can.
